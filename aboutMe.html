about me

I walked into Girls Who Code, like many other girls, wanting to more clearly define my future, and there is no doubt Girls Who Code gives us that opportunity. However, each passing day I do more than merely choose my career path. The most valuable opportunity Girl Who Code gives me was the opportunity not to just push boundaries, surround myself with friendship, or dare to defy expectations, but rather the opportunity to create myself and my future. I take risks. I am brave. Nothing makes me happier to say that I am learning to code and continuing to discover ways to create and innovate. Through code, I grow and, today, I am still growing.
